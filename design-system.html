Cards

Containers for grouped content. Three variants — default, elevated, and tinted — for different levels of visual hierarchy.

Default Card
With header and footer

A standard card with a visible border and white background. Use for primary content containers throughout the interface.

Elevated Card

Tinted Card

A warm stone tint. Ideal for highlighted content, featured callouts, or tips that need gentle distinction.
